Privacy Policy

Information Collected

May List does not collect or store user's personal information. The App operates entirely locally on your device and does not access user data outside the device.

Use of Information

Since May List does not collect user data, your personal information will not be used for any purposes. The App aims to provide a secure and clean user experience without involving the exchange of personal information.

Access Permissions

May List does not require special access permissions to user's personal information. The App only uses necessary permissions for basic functionality like saving task lists on your device.

No Data Transmission to Servers

May List does not transmit user data to external servers. All data entered and stored within the application remains entirely on the user's device and is not accessed by third parties.

Children's Privacy

May List is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
